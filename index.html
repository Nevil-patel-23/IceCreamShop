Our Story

Scoops & Smiles began in 2010 when two childhood friends decided to turn their passion for ice cream into a business. What started as a small stand at the local farmer's market has grown into the beloved shop you see today.

We still make all our ice cream in small batches using milk from local dairy farms and seasonal ingredients from nearby growers.
